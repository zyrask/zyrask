About Me

From a young age, I’ve always been captivated by the digital world. Whether it was designing my first Minecraft structure or scripting my first game in Roblox, I was hooked. I began experimenting with coding at 12, and since then, it’s become my passion. I’ve built everything from multiplayer RPGs to complex in-game systems, constantly pushing my skills further.

When I'm not creating, I'm usually tweaking a new recipe, baking cookies or cakes, or just gaming with friends. My work is a blend of creativity and technical precision, a balance that defines who I am as both a developer and a gamer.

Noteworthy Projects

  • **Project Eclipse** (Roblox RPG Game) A massive multiplayer RPG where players explore a world of magic and mystery. Currently private, but it was a top hit in 2024 before it was retired.
  • **BlockTech** (Minecraft Redstone System) A fully functional in-game computer system built within Minecraft using Redstone and command blocks. One of the most complex builds I’ve done, with full automation and AI capabilities. Was privated due to ongoing development.
  • **ChronoVerse** (Roblox Game – Racing) A time-bending racing game where players race across different eras—past, present, and future. Was a finalist in the Roblox Game Jam 2023, but removed for a major update.
  • **Baking Bot AI** (AI Project) An AI I coded to help guide new bakers through recipes, adjusting based on available ingredients. A fun side project that mixed coding with my love of baking!
